The Problem

Urban infrastructure issues go unreported & unresolved

Cluj-Napoca's citizens encounter daily infrastructure problems — but reporting them is slow, fragmented, and produces no visible action.

🕳️

No easy channel

Citizens call hotlines or post on social media with no systematic follow-up.

📋

Manual categorisation

Every report must be triaged by hand — causing delays and misrouting.

📍

No location tracking

Problems lack GPS context, making dispatch and prioritisation harder.

🔇

Zero transparency

Citizens never know if their report was received, acted on, or resolved.

📊

No data for city hall

Without aggregate data, authorities can't plan maintenance budgets effectively.

⚡

Critical issues missed

Potholes, water leaks, and broken lights stay hazardous for weeks.

Our Solution

One app. AI at the core. City-wide impact.

Cluj Civic AI connects citizens directly to local government through an intelligent, photo-first reporting platform.

1

📸 Snap a photo

Citizen photographs a problem — pothole, broken light, water leak — with their phone.

2

🤖 Claude Vision analyses it instantly

AI classifies the issue type, severity (LOW→CRITICAL), and writes a human-readable summary in seconds.

3

🗺️ GPS-pinned & submitted

The report is geo-tagged and routed to government officers with full context already attached.

4

✅ Track resolution in real time

Citizens follow status updates; officers log actions; the city gets aggregate analytics.

The AI Layer

Powered by Claude Vision API

We pass the uploaded image directly to Claude claude-sonnet-4-6 with a structured prompt — and get back validated JSON instantly.

What Claude returns:
{
  "issueType": "POTHOLE",
  "severity": "HIGH",
  "summary": "Deep 30 cm pothole on roadway, risk of vehicle damage",
  "confidence": 91,
  "additionalNotes": "Temporary marking recommended"
}
12 Issue Types

Pothole · Broken Road · Sidewalk · Overflowing Bin · Garbage · Street Light · Traffic Light · Water Leak · Traffic Sign · Construction · Road Crack · Other

4 Severity Levels

LOW cosmetic · MEDIUM weeks · HIGH hazard · CRITICAL same-day response required

Graceful Fallback

Demo mode with deterministic hash-based scenarios when API is unavailable — app never breaks.
